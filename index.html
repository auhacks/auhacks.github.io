About

CTRL ALT HACKS 1.0

Ctrl Alt Hacks 1.0 is the very first Technology festival organized by the School of Engineering and Technology, Ansal University. Started with the aim of providing a platform where Indian student community can develop and showcase their technical prowess.

Much Awaited Technical Fest

The festival is an Inter-College affair packed to the brim with events like a fast-paced and gruelling National Level Hackathon, a cutthroat E-Gaming Competition, Workshops that are designed based on current industry trends and draw on the rich experience of instructors, a DJ night filled with bass and beats, and much more.

With something to offer for everyone, Ctrl Alt Hacks 1.0 is the ideal destination for all: young or old, beginner or expert, student or professional to witness, learn and experience the wonders of science and technology. So grab your friends, mark your calendars, and gear up for Ansal University’s very first Science and Technology Festival. Welcome to Ctrl Alt Hacks 1.0!

Hackathon 2k19

Smart Energy

With an increasing population and even greater resources utilisation, a dystopian future is not so far away if a blind eye is turned to the current situation at hand. Transform the field of resource consumption to ensure a sustainable environment for the future.

Open Innovation

Be the change you want to see. Without limitations or any restrictions based on any particular field, this track is provided to let you think out of the box and develop an application of your own choice which you think the world needs today.

Internet Of Things

The Internet of things can be basically defined as the extension of Internet connectivity into physical devices and everyday objects. Through this particular track, explore the backbone of connectivity through an interconnected network of data exchange.

Mobility

Horribly maintained roads and gross traffic mismanagement has always been a sight for sore eyes. Figure out new ways to refine transportation facilities and improve daily means of commute. With strategic ideas and proper implementation, travelling does not have to be a nightmare anymore.

Agriculture

In a country where agriculture is a primary occupation, the benefits or advantages for farmers are either ignored or poorly implemented. You get to decide how to incorporate technology and innovation through a traditional livelihood to ensure proper accessibility and availability for the backbone of the nation.
