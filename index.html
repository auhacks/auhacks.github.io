About

CTRL ALT HACKS 1.0

Ctrl Alt Hacks 1.0 is the very first Technology festival organized by the School of Engineering and Technology, Ansal University. Started with the aim of providing a platform where Indian student community can develop and showcase their technical prowess.

Much Awaited Technical Fest

The festival is an Inter-College affair packed to the brim with events like a fast-paced and gruelling National Level Hackathon, a cutthroat E-Gaming Competition, Workshops that are designed based on current industry trends and draw on the rich experience of instructors, a DJ night filled with bass and beats, and much more.

With something to offer for everyone, Ctrl Alt Hacks 1.0 is the ideal destination for all: young or old, beginner or expert, student or professional to witness, learn and experience the wonders of science and technology.
